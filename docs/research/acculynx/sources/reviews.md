Method notes: All sources public; no login, trial, or paywalled surface accessed; no access controls circumvented. Rows marked "via search snippet" were extracted from search-engine result summaries of the listed URL because the direct fetch was bot-blocked (HTTP 403/404) or JS-rendered — the pages themselves were not scraped. Reddit is unreachable from this environment (reddit.com blocked); all Reddit sentiment is secondhand via aggregators and marked accordingly. YouTube comments are JS-rendered and were not retrievable; videos are logged by title/metadata only. Verbatim quotes were taken only from customer-authored reviews, never from AccuLynx-authored material.
