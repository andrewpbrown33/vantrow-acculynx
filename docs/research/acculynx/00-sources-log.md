# 00 — Master Sources Log (Clean-Room Provenance Record)

**Compiled:** 2026-07-15 (individual source access dates span 2026-07-15 to 2026-07-16, as recorded per row).

This document is the master provenance record for all AccuLynx competitive research in this
repository, maintained under `docs/legal/clean-room-protocol.md`. Every factual claim in the
research docs (`docs/research/acculynx/`) must be traceable to an entry here (URL + access
date + what was extracted); claims that cannot be sourced are labeled ASSUMPTION or INFERENCE
inline in the research docs, per protocol §2.

**Scope and method (protocol §1):** All sources are public — marketing sites, public help
centers, public API documentation, press releases, review sites, job postings, DNS/HTTP
metadata, and trade press. No research was conducted from inside an AccuLynx account (trial
or paid); no authenticated or paywalled surface was scraped; no technical access control was
circumvented. Where a public page bot-blocked direct fetching (e.g., `support.acculynx.com`
returns HTTP 403 to non-browser clients; G2, Trustpilot, and YouTube watch pages likewise),
the affected rows are marked as snippet-sourced and their claims are limited to what
search-result summaries showed. The lookalike domain `www-acculynx.com` (hyphen, not dot)
repeatedly surfaced in search results and was deliberately never used as a source.

**Structure:** One section per research module below, keyed to the fragment files in
`sources/` (which remain in place as the per-module working logs). Each section reproduces
that fragment's source table verbatim, including its method/access notes.
